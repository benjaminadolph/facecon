FACECON INTERACTION GUIDE

It is important that the face is well lit and the camera is placed straight to the face with a slight bottom view.

open_with | pointer

As soon as you start the camera, the face pointer will be activated. You can control it with your face movements. For example: If you turn your face to the left, the pointer will move to the left side of the screen. ItÂ´s as easy as that!

touch_app | click

Activate the click button and smile to click on an object below the pointer. The pointer gives you feedback by changing color from blue to red if a click event is performed.

swap_vert looks_one | whole page scroll

Activate the scroll button version 1 and raise your eyebrows to activate the scroll function v1. The activated scroll mode can be recognized by the fact that the icons in the button change color from white to blue. As soon as you move the pointer to the upper or lower half of the screen, you scroll up or down. The closer you get to the edge of the screen, the faster the scrolling speed becomes.

swap_vert looks_two | zone scroll

Activate the scroll button version 2. As soon as you move the pointer into the marked zones at the top or bottom edge of the screen, you scroll up or down. The closer you get to the edge of the screen, the faster the scrolling speed becomes.

code looks_one | wink navigation

Activate the navigation button version 1. To navigate to previous or next page on the website, wink with the corresponding eye.

code looks_two | roll navigation

Activate the navigation button version 2. To navigate to previous or following page on the website, roll your head to the right or left side.
